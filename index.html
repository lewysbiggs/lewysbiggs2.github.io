Welcome to Our Mentoring Program

Are you an aspiring medic? Or perhaps you're already a student eager to pay it forward? Our peer-to-peer mentoring program is tailored to connect current medical students at Swansea Medical School with those who aspire to join our ranks. Whether you need interview assistance, application proofreading, or simply general guidance, our aim is to nurture hopeful individuals. Click the relevant button below to find out more.

Mentor Application

As a mentor, you will be assigned 1 to 3 aspiring medics (at your discretion) who will be able to contact you via WhatsApp or email. The WAMS scheme is simply in place to connect mentors and mentees, so it is entirely your prerogative how and when you engage with your mentees.

We understand you are all busy so we must emphasise there will be no formal commitments. We may offer some resources and guidance to help in your role. If you are interested and keen to engage with the scheme, please apply below!

Mentee Application

As a mentee you will be preferentially assigned to a medic mentor. The scheme will match and put you in contact with a current medical student. From this point on there will be no formal input from WAMS, so it is your prerogative how and when to engage with the mentor. You can always contact us at WAMS with any queries.

If you are interested apply below!

About Us

Swansea WAMS is a student-led society dedicated to assisting aspiring medical students across Wales in various aspects of their applications. Supported by Swansea University Medical School, we maintain a strong partnership with their Admissions Team. Our mission is to provide comprehensive support to future medical professionals, offering guidance and resources tailored to their needs and aspirations..

In addition to our collaboration with Swansea University Medical School, Swansea WAMS offers a range of valuable resources and opportunities to our members. From mock interviews and personal statement workshops to networking events with healthcare professionals, we strive to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary for success in their medical school applications. Our inclusive community fosters collaboration and peer support, creating a conducive environment for growth and development among aspiring medical professionals in the local area and beyond.
